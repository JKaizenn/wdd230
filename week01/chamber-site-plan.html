Rexburg Idaho Chamber of Commerce Site Plan

Site Name

Site Name: Rexburg Idaho Chamber of Commerce

Reason for selecting this name: This name was chosen because it accurately reflects the purpose of promoting the local economy and businesses in Rexburg, Idaho.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is:

  • To promote local businesses and provide a platform for connecting the community with businesses and services in Rexburg.
  • To provide up-to-date information on local events, government policies, and economic opportunities in Rexburg.

Scenarios

These scenarios reflect common questions visitors may ask:

  • What events are the Rexburg Chamber hosting this month that promote networking between local businesses?
  • Where can I find contact information for the board members of the Rexburg Chamber of Commerce?
  • What are the current statistics on business growth in Rexburg?

Color Schema

The following colors will be used across the site:

  • Dusty Blue - Used for headings and background elements.
  • Soft Beige - Applied to body text, paragraphs, and accent colors.

Typography

The selected fonts for the site are:

  • Roboto for general body text, ensuring readability and modern design.
  • Lora for headings, providing a serif contrast for elegant, eye-catching titles.
